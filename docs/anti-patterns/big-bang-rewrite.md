# Anti-Pattern: Big Bang Rewrite

## The Idea

Replace the entire legacy system with a new system in one go.

---

## Why Organizations Choose This

- Frustration with legacy systems
- Belief that "starting fresh" is easier
- Vendor-driven narratives

---

## Why It Fails

- Underestimation of complexity
- Loss of domain knowledge
- Long delivery timelines
- No incremental value delivery

---

## Typical Outcome

- Budget overruns
- Delayed releases
- Business disruption
- Partial or complete project failure

---

## Better Alternative

Use **evolutionary modernization**:

- Strangler Fig pattern
- Incremental service extraction
- Parallel system operation

---

## Key Insight

> Big bang rewrites fail not because of technology,  
> but because of **misunderstanding system complexity**.
